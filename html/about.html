About Me

Let's get a little more personal.

I enjoy trips to the beach, casual shopping, taking pictures of things that I love, and being in the sun. My favorite movie is Les Miserables, my favorite book is "The Sun is Also a Star", and my favorite food is meatloaf and mashed potatoes.

Hawai'i has been where I grew up my whole life. It is my home, my roots, and everything that I love. The beauty of the land, the culture, the people, and the history will always be something I am extremely passionate about. My goal is to continue to learn about the Hawaiian culture, and the language, to be able to use both in my life.



My family is the most important thing in the world to me. I have family by blood, but also by choice, and in my life both are equally as important. These people have made me everything that I am, and everything that I will continue to be. They have taught me patience, perserverance, and above all, unconditional love.
